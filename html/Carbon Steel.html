There are three categories in Carbon Steel, Which Carbon Steel are you Looking for:

Low carbon steel is a type of steel that has small carbon content, typically in the range of 0.05% to 0.3%. Its reduced carbon content makes it more malleable and ductile than other steel types.Low carbon steel is also known as mild steel.

Medium carbon steel has carbon content of 0.25-0.60 wt.% and manganese content of 0.60-1.65 wt.%.The mechanical properties of steel are improved via heat treatment involving autenitising followed by tempering.

Any steel with a carbon content of 0.55 percent or higher, or about one part in 180, is considered high-carbon steel.High carbon steel properties include a very high strength, extreme hardness and resistance to wear, and moderate ductility.
